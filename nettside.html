Outer Wilds

'
Outer wilds is a first person space exploration game. In outer wilds you are stuck in a time loop.
Where you explore a tiny galaxy until time runs out or you die for another reason.
When you die the cycle repeats istself, but you retain all your knowledge from previous time loops.
In outer wilds you are constantly on a timer. If the timer runs out, a supernova occurs(the sun explodes) and you die.

Outer wild is a pretty good game in my book. The artstyle is good. The gameplay is stressful but still fun.
The game is basically a dectetive game in space. The whole game has generally a certain vibe of mystery and calmness.
Each of the 10 planets are very unique and interesting to explore. As you continue to explore you learn new things about the galaxy.
Which will teach how to get past certain obstacles.
It is techically possible to beat the game within an hour or so, but you need information to get past certain areas.
I have not finished the game yet, but i will evntually.
The game is a 9/10 for me.
